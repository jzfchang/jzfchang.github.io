About Me

MIT Architecture Student, Designer & Illustrator

I am a senior in MIT Architecture, Design and Computation department. My passion for design and the human experience comes out into my practical, project-based work.

I have a long standing interest in narratives, within art and writing, and how to express these narratives within different mediums. My background in academic world history combined with emerging technologies for architectural and urban development has led me to my interdisciplinary style of work.
